Wecome to Cambodia

Cambodia has the best of South-East Asia’s magnificent temples and monuments, beautiful warm beaches and its charming people. There's a magic about this charming yet confounding kingdom that casts a spell on visitors.

History

Cambodian ancient civilization known as Khmer Empire which flourished for over 600 years. It allowed successive kings to control and accumulate immense power and wealth. Cambodia is slowly recovering from the horrors of the Khmer Rouge’s reign of terror.

Cuisine

Cambodian or Khmer food is typically made with fresh ingredients and is a medley of flavors. The food has an influence from neibour countries. However, unlike Thai cuisine that is distinctly spicy, the food in Cambodia is rather mild and has a lot of pickled and tangy flavors.
