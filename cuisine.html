Khmer Cuisine

Khmer cuisine is the traditional cuisine of the people of Cambodia. Water, rice and freshwater fish exert the most profound influences on Khmer cuisine. Khmer cuisine shares many commonalities with the food of neighbouring Thailand. Although, less chilli, sugar and coconut cream are used for flavor and of neighboring Vietnam, as well as a colonial history, as both formed part of the French colonial empire in Southeast Asia.
